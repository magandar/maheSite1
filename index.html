Why come here?

Every once in a while we think "What's so special in my motorcycle?" or "What's so special in my car?" Why do you think we do that? Oh! By the way, don't get me wrong, I love bicycles too, sometimes even more than motorcycles or cars, considering the climate change that's haunting our mother Earth. So I definitely didn't mean to leave that out, as one of our go to rides, but I did leave that out for this context alone and you'll get to know why, soon enough.

So circling back to the question, why do you think we do that? Let it be apparels, shoes or even a watch, we always tend to have one too many than we really need. If you know why, then you already have the answer to our questions, because it is for the same reason those questions pop up in our head. As human beings we are always unique and by having multiple items we are customizing our closet to better reflect oneself. So we think about what is special or if not, what we can do to make our vehicle special, to be unique, to perform better, to suit us better, to reflect us. You cannot go about buying tens of cars and motorcycles, because that not only digs into you savings, even if you are a millionaire but its just bad to our environment and ofcourse you can do that with a bicycle though. This opens the door to MODS which is the just cooler version of modifications.

By now, you must have guessed why you are here and what we are going to do as a team. But still, let me put it out there. I would like us to share our experiences with different mods and help each other along the way to educate ourselves to becaome better DIYers with respect to mods. It's that simple! So what are you waiting for? Sign Up and lets do it!
